cells

definitions/functions

nucleus

The brain of the cell.

vacuole

A place to store energy.

cytoplasm

A jelly-like fluid to fill up extra space.

ribosome

Gives protein to the cell overtime.

mitochondria

Provides energy to the cell. (Photosynthesis)

cell wall

A wall to protect the cell (only in plant cells).

cell membrane

It decides what comes in and out of the cell.

chloroplast

Contains Chlorophyll which helps in photosynthesis.

organelles

The main important things inside a living cell.

unicellular

1 Cell organism.

multicellular

More than 1 cell organism.

types of Cells

plant cells:
Nucleus, Vacuole, Cytoplasm, Ribosome, Mitochondria, Cell Wall, Cell Membrane,

animal cells:
Nucleus, Vacuole(small/Doesn't have), Cytoplasm, Ribosome, Mitochondria, Cell Membrane,
