variables

independent variable

the variable that is altered during a scientific experiment (changes).


dependent variable

the variable being tested or measured during a scientific experiment.(Measured)


controlled variable

a variable that is kept the same during a scientific experiment.(Same)

solution components

solute

E.g. Sugar.

definition: The substance that dissolves in a solution. In this case, sugar is the solute.

solvent

E.g. Water.

definition: The substance that dissolves the solute. In this case, water is the solvent.
