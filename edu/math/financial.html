types of pay

salary

a fixed amount per year usaly paid weekly or monthly

wage

a fixed ammount for each hour worked

commission

a percentage of the value of goods ir servuces sold is paid.

picework

a fixed ammount for each item produced or completed

fee

a fixed amount for a service provided

casual

a fixed hourly rate for the number of hours worked, any time.

gross & net pay

gross pay is the amount of money an employee makes

net pay is the amount of money after taxes are taken out

overtime

overtime is when a employee works when their normal hours are over.

time and a half is when someone is paid 1.5x their normal ammount for the time worked over normal hours.
double time is when someone is paid 2x their normal ammount for the time worked over normal hours.
