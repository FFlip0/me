to wish misery

To feel the urge,
To feel the idea.
To view them,
At their worst

To change the way they think,
And watch them fall

To break the land upon their feet
And watch them fall

To exploit their trust,
And watch them fall
To feel their thoughts

To minpulate them on a level they can't comprehend
To use themselves against them
To break the unspoken laws of this world

To see yourself and speak of envy.
